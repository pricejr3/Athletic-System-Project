Miami University Medical Form Instructions

Welcome to the Miami University Sports Medicine Intranet. Please fill out the forms you were instructed to in your welcome letter. The steps for each form are:

  1. Select the form in the drop-down list and click the "Fill Out New Form" button.
  2. Fill out the form COMPLETELY (if there are multiple sections for information - PLEASE FILL IT OUT) and click the "Submit" button at the bottom of the page.
  3. If you are a Veteran Student-Athlete or a Freshmen/Transfer Student-Athlete then please fill out the appropriate forms listed/assigned below:
    Veteran Student-Athlete:
    1. Athlete Release Form
    2. FERPA Release Form
    3. Health Appraisal Form
    4. Health Information Release Form
    5. Insurance Form
    6. Waiver Form
    7. Declaration of Injury
    8. ADHD Medical Exception Form
    9. Nutritional/Menstrual Form (Female only)
    10. Mental Health Form
    		    Freshmen/Transfer Student-Athlete:
    1. Athlete Release Form
    2. FERPA Release
    3. Health Appraisal Form
    4. Health Information Release Form
    5. Insurance Form
    6. Waiver Form
    7. Family History Form
    8. Health History Form
    9. Declaration of Injury
    10. ADHD Medical Exception Form
    11. Nutritional/Menstrual Form (Female only)
    12. Mental Health Form
  4. If you see a message stating "Form could not be submitted due to the following reasons:", then please read the warnings/errors listed in red and make the appropriate corrections on the form. Then click the "Submit" button at the bottom of the page.
  5. Once the form has passed verification, you will be asked to review it. Please double check all of your responses then click the "Insert Form Data into Database" button at the bottom of the page. If you would like to change any of the information, then click on the "Edit Form" button instead.
  6. If successful, then you will see a message stating that the "Database insertion was successful".
  7. To see the updated list of completed forms, click on the "Refresh Form List" link at the top of the page.
  8. Please note that you may make modifications to your information for the designated time that you have access to the system.
  9. Once forms have all FINAL modifications to them, please PRINT (may need to increase margins and/or set to landscape in the page set) each form off and SIGN/DATE in all the appropriate places (if you are under the age of 18 please have your legal guardian/parent sign in all the appropriate places). To print a form, click on it in your list of forms, then click the "Print" link at the bottom of the form.
  10. After forms are PRINTED and SIGNED/DATED please mail them to or drop them off at:
    Miami University Sports Medicine
    Attn: Dr. Stephen Dailey
    144 Yager Stadium
    Oxford, OH 45056
