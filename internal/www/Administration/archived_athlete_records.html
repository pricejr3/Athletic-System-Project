Once athlete records are archived, they are to remain in the database for a period of 7 years following the archiving. After this time, they may be purged from the database permanently. This utility permits an Intranet user with ADMIN clearance to do precisely this. Note that even after an athlete's record is purged, it will still exist on the backup tapes, and therefore can be recovered in the event of an emergency or a mistake.
